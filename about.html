About US

Including the rarest of colors; retail, or lucrative returns on investment; Ikon Global established as the Diamond Manufacturer, Trader and has the expertise to transcend all aspects of the operations, involved in the manufacturing, and investment of Diamonds.

Company Profile

Icon Global is a team with good working experience and having presence in Latin America, Ghana and other region of Africa. Leading in import and export of Gold, Silver and Diamond Jewellery. Also having Bullion experience since last 10 years.
