Gold and Silver Jewelry

Diamond jewelry has been the symbolic expression of love and ever-lasting commitment because of its endurance and sparkling brilliance. Our designers and skilled jewelry makers at Ikon Global put their heart and soul to create exceptional and remarkable timepieces that become the perfect expression of your bond.

JEWELRY REPAIR & RESTORATION

Diamond jewelry worn regularly can be exposed to wear and tear or damage and require more than one restoration and repair services. Therefore, our talented craftsmen specialize in various restoration and repair techniques to ensure the optimal maintenance of your most valuable pieces.

We offer diamond recutting and polishing, jewelry engraving, stone readjustment, restoring misplaced diamonds, and jewelry preservation services at the advanced Goldiama Diamond lab and factory after an in-depth evaluation and consultation with you.

If you are looking for expert restoration and repair services for your most cherished diamond jewelry, then book an appointment with us today, and we will be delighted to repair it with utmost precision and bring it back to its immaculate state.

CREATE YOUR BRAND

As a well-established company, we believe in enabling other members in the retail sector of the diamond jewelry market to grow and achieve success.

Apart from the wide array of services, Ikon Global offers the convenience of high-end diamond jewelry manufacturing services to the designers, inclined towards establishing their brands in the retail industry.

With our best-in-class craftspeople, a diamond lab, and a factory that houses the latest Sarine and GIA technology, we execute the diamond manufacturing processes, seamlessly. If you are looking to establish your diamond jewelry brand, then our expert team is available to cater to your requirement.
