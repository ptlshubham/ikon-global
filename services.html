DIAMOND TRADING

Our success lies in offering the finest and responsibly sourced diamonds at competitive prices along with value-added services. The diamonds we source, entail Kimberley Process Certification and have been sourced as per the conflict-free standards and compliance policies.

Over the period of years, we have built a sustainable web of associates and partners, established a strong global footprint by trading millions of carats per year, developed long-term client relationships, and offered our clients the ease of availing diamond trading services from across the globe, anywhere.

We deal in Kimberley Certified rough and GIA certified polished diamonds, and fancy diamonds. We cater to the diamond producers and buyers; including but not limited to high-end jewelry producers, diamond brokers, manufacturers, and individuals, interested in purchasing and selling loose and polished diamonds. Hence, today, we are the one of the leading and trusted trading partners for many of our clients as we facilitate them with tailored services.

Our experienced trading specialists are always abreast of the latest and ongoing diamond market trends, enabling our clients to achieve success every day.

FANCY DIAMONDS RANGE

Bullion Bars

Gold Bullion Bars are the most recognised form of bullion purchase and are suitable for most investment goals. We stock a wide range of competitively priced products from different manufacturers around the world giving the flexibility to diversify your investment portfolio whilst owning beautiful items.

If you cannot source a specific product or you need any help in deciding which product is right for you then please get in touch and a member of our customer service team will be happy to help.

Gold Coins

Gold Bullion Coins are increasingly seen as appealing alternatives to Gold bars due to the variety of designs, sizes and countries of origin that are available. This variety has introduced an additional level of interest into Precious Metal Investments by providing the opportunity to assemble collections of interesting items whilst still making investment purchases.

Dore

Our Dubai-based team procures Gold from high quality Gold Mines worldwide. The prudent compliance policies of Ikon Global enables us to source from reputed Gold Mines across the globe. Our biggest market for re-export/sale of Gold Dore bars is India
