MANUFACTURING BRILLIANT & SCINTILLATING DIAMONDS

We feature unprecedented symmetry and supreme accuracy while exuding remarkable radiance that is simply unmatched. We hand-pick rough diamonds, analyze the potential in each, and then transform them into the finest diamonds that fulfill the highest grading standards of GIA.

Our experience leads us to produce exceptional quality products. Being one of the leading manufacturers in the GCC region, we have our in-house, high-tech manufacturing laboratory and factory where rough diamonds are polished every day on a large-scale. This results in a high annual turnover, catering to the demand for the sought-after diamonds in the industry.

DIAMOND CERTIFICATION

We excel at manufacturing diamonds, exuding brilliance, and fire. Each diamond we polish and manufacture meets the highest grading standards of GIA (Gemological Institute Of America) Certification. With our unmatched expertise, years of experience, and a highly-skilled multinational team of professionals, we execute the manufacturing processes accurately to produce the finest diamonds.
